Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What We Collect

  • Email address - for authentication (magic link login)
  • Birth year - for age verification (COPPA compliance), never displayed publicly
  • Profile information - display name, bio, avatar, mood, music (all optional, all public)

How We Use It

Your email is used only for authentication. Your birth year is used only to verify you are 13 or older. Profile information and posts are displayed on the platform as part of the service.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS). We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Content Moderation

Posts are checked by automated moderation (OpenAI API) to enforce community guidelines. Post content is sent to OpenAI for moderation only and is not used for training.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication only. No tracking or analytics cookies are used.

Your Rights

You can delete your posts at any time. To delete your account entirely, contact us at the email below.
