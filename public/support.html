Common questions

Are my entries private? Yes — every entry is private by default. An entry only becomes visible to others if you deliberately make it public and publish your public profile page.

How do I delete my account or export my data? Open Settings inside the app. You can export all of your data or permanently delete your account and everything in it, right away — no email required.

How do I report or block someone? Every public post has a report button and a block button. Reports come straight to us and we review them promptly.

How old do I have to be? You must be at least 13 years old to use Retrowave Journal.
