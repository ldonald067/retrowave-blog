❓ common questions

are my entries private?

yes! every entry is private by default. it only becomes visible to others if you deliberately make it public and publish your profile page.

how do i delete my account or export my data?

open Settings in the app. you can export all of your data, or permanently delete your account and everything in it right away, no email required.

how do i report or block someone?

every public post has a report button and a block button. reports come straight to us and we review them promptly.

how old do i have to be?

you must be at least 13 years old to use Retrowave Journal.
