Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What We Collect

  • Email address - for authentication (account sign-in, confirmation, and recovery emails)
  • Birth year - for age verification (COPPA compliance), never displayed publicly
  • Profile information - display name, bio, avatar, mood, music, and public-page settings
  • Journal entries and reactions - private by default, with optional public sharing when you choose

How We Use It

Your email is used only for authentication. Your birth year is used only to verify you are 13 or older. Journal entries stay private unless you intentionally make an entry public and publish your public page.

Data Storage & Service Providers

We never sell your data. To run the service we use a small set of processors: Supabase (database and authentication, hosted on AWS), Cloudflare (website hosting and email forwarding), Resend (delivery of authentication emails), OpenAI (automated content moderation only), and DiceBear (generates your cartoon avatar image from your account ID). Each receives only the minimum data needed for its function.

Content Moderation

Posts are checked by automated moderation (OpenAI API) to enforce community guidelines. Post content is sent to OpenAI for moderation only and is not used for training.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use essential browser storage (cookies/local storage) for authentication and draft autosaving only. No tracking or analytics.

Your Rights

You can delete your posts at any time. From Settings you can export all of your data or permanently delete your account and everything in it — immediately, no email required. For anything else, contact us below.

Contact

For privacy questions, email: support@retrowaveblog.com
